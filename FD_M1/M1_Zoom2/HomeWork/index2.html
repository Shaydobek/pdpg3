Sangardak waterfall

One of the pearls of Uzbekistan is considered the incredible beauty of the Sangardak waterfall. The waterfall is located 50 km to the west of Denau. It is a small town, the district center in Surkhandarya region in a picturesque gorge.

Unlike many other waterfalls, in Sangardak the water doesnâ€™t falls as one stream, it has number of individual jets. The water here flows out under the pressure of karst waters from numerous holes in the high rock. The rocks at the waterfall are covered with emerald moss and grass all year round .The smallest splashes of the waterfall sparkle in the sun and form incredible rainbows. It is a fantastically fascinating sight.
